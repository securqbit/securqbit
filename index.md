## What Securqbit is

Securqbit is a mobile VPN (iOS & Android) engineered to get through strict firewalls. Obfuscated traffic, zero logs, modern cryptography, and a default‑on kill switch — built by people who actually live behind firewalls.

The cryptography is the same stack your browser and SSH client rely on. What's novel is **how we survive hostile networks without compromising any of it.**

## Who it's for

- People on networks that block or throttle ordinary VPNs.
- Anyone who wants real privacy — no logs they could be compelled to hand over.
- Travelers and remote workers on weak, congested, or captive networks (3G, hotel Wi‑Fi, public hotspots).
- Users who want a native, battery‑aware app instead of a heavyweight client.

## Why it's different

> **Boring reliability. Quiet privacy. No drama.**

- **It works behind firewalls.** Obfuscated transport plus adaptive protocol hopping means deep packet inspection just sees ordinary HTTPS. If an entry point is blocked in your country, the app rotates to one that isn't.
- **It can't betray you.** There are no activity logs to leak, subpoena, or sell — the servers run on volatile storage and reboot to a clean state.
- **It stays out of the way.** Connect in under a second on most networks, with battery overhead typically under 2% per day.
