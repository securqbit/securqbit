# Privacy Policy

🔗 **Live:** [securqbit.com/privacy](https://securqbit.com/privacy)

*Effective date: 2026-02-18*

This Privacy Policy explains how Securqbit (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use our websites, applications, and VPN services (“Services”).

We aim to collect as little data as possible and to be clear about what we do collect.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide

- Account information (such as email) if you create an account
- Support communications you send us

### 1.2 Information collected to operate the service

We may collect limited operational data needed to:

- maintain service stability and prevent abuse
- troubleshoot reliability issues
- secure our systems

Examples may include:

- service errors and diagnostic logs from the app (if enabled or provided)
- high-level service events (e.g., connection success/failure counts)

We do **not** collect browsing history or traffic contents.

### 1.3 Website analytics (optional)

If we use analytics, we will prefer privacy-preserving options. If enabled, analytics may collect basic page view information (e.g., page visited, device type). We do not sell analytics data.

---

## 2. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not log:

- websites you visit
- DNS queries content (beyond what is required for service operation, where applicable)
- traffic contents
- messages, files, or content you transmit

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide and maintain the Services
- improve performance and reliability
- respond to support requests
- protect against abuse and attacks

---

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal data.

We may share information:

- with service providers that help us run the Services (e.g., infrastructure hosting), under confidentiality obligations
- if required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and security

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it where feasible.

---

## 6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No system is 100% secure, but we design our infrastructure with security as a priority.

---

## 7. Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of account information, subject to legal requirements.

---

## 8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above.
